Easy Pancake

Ingridients Amount
flour 1 cup
sugar 2 Tbsp.
baking powder 2 tsp.
salt 1/4 tsp.
eggs 2
milk 1 cup
vegetable oil 1/4 cup

Instruction

  1. In a large bowl whisk the dry ingredients together.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients including the egg.
  3. Next, heat a griddle/large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour your pancake batter onto a heated surface.
  5. When the pancake forms bubbles and starts to pop, flip the pancakes.
  6. Cook until done and top with a small tab of butter.
  7. Serve warm smothered in maple syrup, and enjoy!
